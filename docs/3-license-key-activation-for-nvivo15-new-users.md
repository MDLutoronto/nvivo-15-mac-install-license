#### **License Key Activation for NVivo 15 - New Users**
{: #license-key-activation-for-nvivo15-new-users}

1. Activate NVivo by clicking on **"provide enterprise key to activate"**.

    <img src='{{ '/assets/images/launch.JPG' | relative_url }}' alt='Nvivo login with "or provide enterprise key to activate" highlighted' title='' width='781' height='352' />

 

2. Copy and paste the **License Key** received in your email into the license key's text box. Read through and **Agree** to the user acknowledgement of license terms, click **Next.**

    <img src='{{ '/assets/images/7_0.png' | relative_url }}' alt='Nvivo License Terms with agreement option selected' title='' width='1496' height='1154' />

3. Fill in the required information, and click **Activate** to finish activating your license.

    <img src='{{ '/assets/images/8.png' | relative_url }}' alt='License Activation screen with First Name, Last Name, Email Address and Country highlighted' title='' width='1490' height='1214' />

4. Your license has now been activated! Click **OK**

    <img src='{{ '/assets/images/9_0.png' | relative_url }}' alt='License activated confirmation screen' title='' width='512' height='390' />

5.  Finish setting up your User Profile by filling in your name and initials, and click **OK.** Filling in your initials is especially important in team projects, as this information is used to identify your work.

    <img src='{{ '/assets/images/10.png' | relative_url }}' alt='User profile screen with Name and Initials highlighted' title='' width='820' height='432' />

That's it! NVivo should now be running on your Mac.
